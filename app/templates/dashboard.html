Latonya Stevenson-Wright

My name is Latonya Stevenson-Wright. I am 43 years old, and I hail from New Haven, CT, and am currently living in Columbia, SC. I am married and have three children ages 18-21 who are finding their own way in life. I have two dogs, a toy poodle named Sasha and a miniature schnauzer named Bentley. I am a veteran of the US Army as a dental technician. I graduated from the school formerly known as Tunxis Community College in Farmington, CT with an AAS in Dental Hygiene in 2013. I received my Bachelor of Science in Dental Hygiene here at the University of Bridgeport in August 2022. Throughout my career, I have taken on the role of pediatric dental hygienist, working in a public health setting, and volunteering for mission work in countries other than the U.S. dental clinics providing care where needed most (Iraq and Jamaica). Currently, I am managing two roles in this field, in public health at the VA Hospital and in education as adjunct clinical staff at Midlands Technical College, both here in Columbia.
